Searching: We will look up our data to find a requested element is present or not.

Linear Search: #Time-O(N) Space-O(1)
  -Items are searched one-by-one. Items can be sorted or not.
  -It is also called sequential search.
Binary Search:
  -It only works with sorted array.
  -Half of the elements can be eliminated at a time, instead of eliminating them one by one.
